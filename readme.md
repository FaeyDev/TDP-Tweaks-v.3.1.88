## ‚öôÔ∏è Installation & Setup

1. **Download the .zip file of the Magisk module you want to install to your device.**
2. **Open Magisk Manager:**
3. **Launch the Magisk Manager app on your Android device.**
4. **Go to the Modules Section:**
5. **Tap on the "Modules" tab at the bottom of the screen.**
6. **Add the Module:**
7. **In the Modules section, tap the "Install from Storage" button (represented by a plus "+" icon or text, depending on your Magisk version).**
8. **Navigate to the location where you downloaded the Magisk module .zip file.**
9. **Select the .zip file to begin the installation process.**
10. **Wait for the Installation:**
11. **Magisk will flash the module and show the installation log. Wait for the process to complete.**
12. **When it's finished, it should say "Module installed successfully."**
13. **Reboot Your Device:**
14. **After the module is flashed, you will be prompted to reboot your device.**
15. **Tap on "Reboot" to restart the device and apply the changes.**
